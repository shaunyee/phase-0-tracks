taking a shower Pseudocode:
-walk into shower until under shower head
-turn on water to a warm temperature
	-sit under water for 20 seconds
	-when 20 seconds are up, move head out of water range
	-apply shampoo to head
	-When shampoo is fully applied, place head back under water for 10 seconds
	-when 10 seconds are up, move head out of water range
	-apply conditioner to head
	-move body out of water range
	-apply soap to body
	-step back into water
	-wash soap off body for 20 seconds
	-place head back into water range
	-wash conditioner out of hair for 10 seconds
-turn off water
-turn and grab a towel
-dry body with towel for 45 seconds
-exit shower
